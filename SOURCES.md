## Research Notes

### Reliability Assessment

**Primary Sources (1896-1897 newspapers)**:
- Contemporary but potentially sensationalized
- Yellow journalism era — competitive, profit-driven
- Some reporters later admitted fabrication
- Value: Documents cultural phenomenon itself

**Modern Analysis**:
- Bartholomew and Cohen most thorough
- Skeptical Inquirer articles well-researched
- Academic consensus: mass hysteria + hoaxes

**Physical Evidence**:
- None verified from 1896-1897 period
- Aurora investigation found no crash debris
- No photographs (technology limitation)
